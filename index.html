About us

LeadTheWay was founded by an avid traveller who was keen on finding a better solution to enjoying his vacations. Our growing team of engineers work passionately to drive the future of tourism and help the traveling community to stay at the forefront of navigation technology. Today, LeadTheWay has gained recognition as the best traveling buddy all around the globe and has raised over $40M in funding.
